Why Does Everything Need an App?

Why is it that everything need an app from toasters to skateboards? During this talk we will discuss the ramifications of product organizations relying on companion apps and how that effects the user experience of their hardware product. How can we build companion apps that build on top of the already great experience of the product and the struggle that imposing between privacy great experience and business.

From Form Fields to Ship It: How not to Design System

Building a design system is very trendy, but when is it the right time to conceptualize, design and execute on a system for use across a product and an organization. Given the context of both new products and legacy products in the same ecosystem, this talk will explore the lessons learned and some best strategies for building a design system.
